# PlanetAI – AI Powered 3D Digital Twin of Earth

An immersive, futuristic web application that presents a highly interactive 3D Digital Twin of Earth. Explore live environmental, geographical, and space-related data while interacting with an AI assistant that explains events and provides insights.

## Features

- **Interactive 3D Earth** – Rotate, zoom, pan with realistic procedural textures, day/night lighting, clouds, and atmospheric glow
- **Live Weather Layer** – Temperature, rainfall, wind direction/speed, humidity, pressure, and storm locations
- **Earthquake Layer** – Animated glowing markers with magnitude, location, time, depth, and risk level
- **Air Pollution Layer** – AQI visualization with PM2.5, PM10, CO, and NO₂ data using color gradients
- **Wildfire Layer** – Active fire hotspots with intensity, area affected, and detection time
- **Satellite Tracker** – ISS, weather, GPS, and communication satellites orbiting Earth in real time
- **Flight Tracker** – Animated flight paths with origin, destination, speed, and altitude
- **Climate Change Simulator** – Interactive sliders for CO₂, glacier melt, sea level, temperature, and deforestation
- **Natural Disaster Simulator** – Simulate cyclones, hurricanes, floods, earthquakes, tsunamis, and volcanoes
- **AI Earth Assistant** – Chatbot answering questions about natural phenomena, climate, and Earth systems
- **Space View** – Zoom out to explore the Moon, Mars, Sun, and major planets of our solar system
- **Analytics Dashboard** – Live charts for global temperature, CO₂, population, forest cover, ice caps, ocean temperature, and air quality
- **Search** – Search countries, cities, mountains, rivers, oceans, volcanoes, and airports with auto-zoom
- **Timeline** – Move through years (1990–2025) to compare environmental changes

## Technology Stack

- **React** + **TypeScript**
- **Three.js** + **React Three Fiber** (3D rendering)
- **Tailwind CSS** (styling)
- **Framer Motion** (animations)
- **Recharts** (data visualization)
- **Vite** (build tool)
